Artwork permissions made simple for dance class music

Cadance obtains written permission from rights holders to feature album artwork on our App Store listings. Clear scope. Full control. Zero surprises.

Email us about permissions

Used by dance educators worldwide.

Why partner with Cadance

  • Increase exposure to a global community of dance instructors.
  • Controlled usage: artwork appears only in store listings and promotional screenshots.
  • No modifications to your art; no implication of endorsement.
  • Easy, revocable permission letter; we maintain records.

Transparency first

Written permission is required before any artwork is displayed.

Scope: Apple App Store and Google Play product pages, Cadance-owned web properties, press kits.

No resale, no licensing of the artwork to third parties, and no use within the in-app player UI without separate permission.

Revocation: permissions can be revoked; we will remove affected assets promptly.

How it works

  1. Email us the titles/artists you authorize.
  2. We send a one-page permission letter specifying scope, regions, and term.
  3. You approve digitally; we store the record.
  4. Send preferred source artwork; we credit per your guidelines.
