Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from artists considering partnership with Cadance.

What exactly will you do with my music artwork?

+

We use your album artwork solely for app store marketing materials - screenshots showing the app interface, promotional images, and app store listings. Your artwork appears as it would in any music player, demonstrating how dance teachers would see your music in our app. We never modify, crop, or alter your artwork.

Do you need rights to my actual music files?

+

No, we only need permission to display your album artwork in our marketing materials. We don't distribute, stream, or include your actual music files in the app. Dance teachers purchase your music through existing channels like iTunes, Spotify, or your website.

How does this benefit me as an artist?

+

Your music gets exposure to thousands of dance teachers who actively purchase class music. We provide full attribution and, where possible, direct links to purchase your music. It's essentially free marketing to your ideal audience - dance educators who appreciate quality accompaniment music.

Can I withdraw permission later?

+

Absolutely. You can withdraw permission at any time with 30 days notice. We'll remove your artwork from future marketing materials and app store updates. This gives you complete control over how your work is used.

What if I'm based outside the United States?

+

We welcome international artists! Many of our partners are based in Europe, Canada, and other countries. Our agreements comply with international copyright law and GDPR requirements. We handle all the legal complexities of international partnerships.

How long does the partnership process take?

+

For simple email agreements, we can have everything finalized within 1-2 weeks. Formal partnership agreements may take 2-4 weeks depending on any customizations needed. We work at your pace and keep you informed throughout the process.
